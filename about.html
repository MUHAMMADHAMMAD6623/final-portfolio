About Me

Hey there! I'm Hammad, a passionate front-end developer with a love for creating beautiful and functional websites. My journey into the world of web development began with a simple curiosity about how websites work, and over time, it has grown into a deep passion. I'm constantly learning, experimenting, and improving my skills, whether it's building responsive layouts, optimizing performance, or crafting seamless user experiences.

At just 13 years old, I dove headfirst into the exciting world of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, crafting my first websites from scratch. From understanding how to structure web pages to making them visually appealing, I found joy in every line of code I wrote. As I grew, so did my ambition and curiosity, pushing me to explore more advanced tools and technologies. WordPress became a significant part of my toolkit, allowing me to expand into the world of dynamic and content-driven websites, where I learned to create custom themes, optimize user experiences, and offer versatility to businesses.

My Journey includes:

Learning HTML & CSS

I started learning the basics of HTML and CSS at a young age, experimenting with simple websites and layouts. Understanding how structure and style come together was an exciting discovery for me.

Getting Deeper into JavaScript

After mastering the essentials of HTML and CSS, I moved on to JavaScript. This opened up a whole new realm of possibilities—interactivity, dynamic content, and the ability to make websites truly come to life.

Exploring WordPress

As my skills grew, I ventured into WordPress, learning how to create custom themes, design layouts, and manage content efficiently. WordPress allowed me to take static websites to a more dynamic and user-friendly level.

Building React Applications

React was my next big challenge. Learning its component-based structure, hooks, and state management concepts helped me build powerful single-page applications. React brought structure to my projects and allowed me to optimize performance.

Working on Projects

Throughout my journey, I’ve worked on numerous personal projects—ranging from a todo list with localStorage integration, to a password strength checker, to creating dynamic calculators for vinyl and other materials. Each project has contributed to my learning and growth as a developer.
